# Disable Email Confirmation - Quick Steps

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Go to Supabase Dashboard:**
   - Open: https://supabase.com/dashboard/project/xgsncypsczrqgcecmpok/auth/settings

2. **Find Email Settings:**
   - Scroll down to the **"Email Auth"** section
   - Look for **"Enable email confirmations"**

3. **Disable It:**
   - Toggle the switch to **OFF** (gray/unchecked)
   - Click **"Save"** at the bottom

4. **Test:**
   - Sign up a new account
   - You should be automatically logged in without needing email confirmation!

---

## What This Does

- ✅ Users can sign up and immediately log in
- ✅ No waiting for confirmation emails
- ✅ Faster user experience
- ✅ Can re-enable later when ready for production

---
